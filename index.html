Research interests

My research interest lie broadly within the field of numerical analysis and partial differential equations with particular interest in numerical methods for nonlinear conservation laws. More specifically, I am interested in finite volume methods, convergence rates of numerical methods and nonlocal equations.

Current projects include higher-order finite volume methods for nonlocal conservation laws, convergence rates of monotone schemes approximating conservation laws that have fluxes with discontinuous spatial dependency, and numerical methods for nonlinear peridynamic equations.
